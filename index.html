notes from self

Messages I send to myself, privately, using Facebook Messenger's ability to message your own profile. Usually when I am on my phone, on a walk, in transit, or too tired to grab a piece of paper to write something down.

Yes, I know that the Notes app exists.

There is a charm to conversing with oneself. Can there be poetry in the mundane thoughts, PDF files, recipes of modern life?

(Sent between 2024-2026)
